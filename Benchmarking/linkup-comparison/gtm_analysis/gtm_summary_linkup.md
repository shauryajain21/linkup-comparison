## Where Competitors Beat Us

### You.com Advantages:
- **Speed:** 0.70s avg response time (12x faster than Linkup Deep)
- **Simple factual queries:** Capital of France, Snowflake news
- **Real-time news:** Latest company updates, stock prices
- **Media content:** YouTube videos, earnings calls

**When to compete:** Research depth > Speed requirements

### Perplexity Advantages:
- **Current events:** Latest news, earnings, financial data
- **Balanced response time:** 12.50s (similar to Linkup Deep)
- **Market positioning:** Already known for AI-powered search

**Differentiation:** More sources, better for technical/developer queries

### Parallel APIs (Agentic/Oneshot):
- **Extremely comprehensive:** 4,000-7,000 word responses
- **Quantitative queries:** "How many locations/providers"
- **Deep research reports:** Full analysis vs quick answers

**Differentiation:** Balanced depth with source quality
