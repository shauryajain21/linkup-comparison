Linkup API Comparison - Benchmark Results

About This Benchmark

Comprehensive comparison of 7 search APIs across 1,400 queries.

  • APIs Tested: Linkup Standard, Linkup Deep, Perplexity, Exa, You.com, Tavily, Valyu
  • Total Queries: 1,400
  • Metrics: Success rate, response time, source count, answer quality

Key Findings

  • Linkup Standard: 96.8% success rate, 33.7 avg sources (3-6x more than competitors)
  • You.com: 98.2% success rate, fastest at 1.03s average
  • Exa: 97.9% success rate, 3.02s average
  • Perplexity: 97.4% success rate, best answer length (1608 chars)
  • Valyu: 64.9% actual success rate (35% "No results found")
